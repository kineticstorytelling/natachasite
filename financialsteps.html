Financial Steps to Take Before Graduate School


There are many moving parts to consider when planning to enroll in a graduate master's degree program. You will need to determine the type of degree to pursue, determine enrollment status (full time vs part time),figure out the application process and understand your financing options.


Let's start by understanding the Pro's and Con's of a master's degree program

Pro's of a Master's Degree Program

  • Higher Earning Potential
  • Increase Upward Mobility
  • Expand your Leadership Capabilities
  • Gain Specialized Knowledge

Con's of a Master's Degree Program

  • Cost
  • Time Commitment
  • Reduced Income
